# Western Website Builder Marketing Sites -- Layout Pattern Report (2025)

Compiled: 2026-07-14

Sites analyzed: Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Carrd, Duda, Square Online (Weebly), Strikingly, Site123, Zyro (Hostinger), GoDaddy Websites.

---

## 1. Hero Section Structure

### General Trends
- **Split-screen (text left / image right)** is the dominant pattern among high-converting builder landing pages, delivering ~28% better conversion than full-width image overlays.
- **Centered layouts** remain popular for brands emphasizing visual impact and memorability.
- **Shift away from full-screen video backgrounds** toward static split-screen or two-column grid layouts -- driven by load-time and mobile-responsibility concerns.

### Site-by-Site Observations

| Site | Headline Style | Layout | Notable Traits |
|------|---------------|--------|----------------|
| **Wix** | Benefit-driven, outcome-focused | Split-screen | Strong visual mockups of sites built on Wix; Editor X / Wix Studio branding |
| **Squarespace** | "AI-ready website platform" | Split-screen / centered | Emphasizes aesthetics and creative control |
| **Webflow** | Designer-centric messaging | Split-screen | Centered nav bar; showcases interactive/complex site examples |
| **Carrd** | Minimal, understated | Single-column centered | Clean, minimal aesthetic; no heavy imagery in hero |
| **Duda** | "Build websites faster" | Split-screen | B2B/professional tone; speed and scalability emphasis |
| **Square Online / Weebly** | Commerce-integrated messaging | Split-screen | Shopify/Stripe integration visuals prominent |
| **Strikingly** | Bold, full-screen background | Full-screen hero with overlay | Asymmetric layouts; mobile-first responsive approach |
| **Site123** | "easiest free website builder" | Split-screen | Pre-designed template previews; simplicity focus |
| **Zyro (Hostinger)** | "Make a website in minutes" | Split-screen | Time-to-publish as primary value prop |
| **GoDaddy** | "Create your website today" | Split-screen | Action-oriented CTA; integrated hosting/domain upsell |

### Common Elements Across All 10 Sites
- Primary headline (value proposition, under 8 words)
- Sub-headline or supporting sentence
- Prominent CTA button (Sign Up Free / Get Started / Try It Now)
- Secondary CTA (Watch Demo / View Templates)
- Trust signals (user counts, award badges) placed below or beside hero

## 2. Template Gallery Display

### General Trends
- **Grid-based layouts** are universal across all platforms.
- **Filtering by industry, style, and features** is standard.
- **Responsive preview toggles** (desktop/tablet/mobile) are common on premium platforms.

### Site-by-Site Observations

| Site | Gallery Style | Filtering | Preview |
|------|--------------|-----------|---------|
| **Wix** | Advanced masonry/grid | Industry, style, color, page count | Live preview; Editor X responsive editor |
| **Squarespace** | Curated visual grid | Industry, layout type | Block-level editing previews |
| **Webflow** | Advanced grid + interactive previews | Category, style, complexity | Figma import; component-level previews |
| **Carrd** | Compact card grid | Simple, Pro, Portfolio, Landing Page | One-page preview only |
| **Duda** | Professional/business-focused grid | Industry, responsive breakpoint | Multi-device responsive preview |
| **Square Online** | E-commerce focused grid | Store type, product category | Cart/checkout flow preview |
| **Strikingly** | Minimal card grid | Industry, purpose | Single-column scroll preview |
| **Site123** | Template thumbnails with labels | Industry, style | Static preview |
| **Zyro** | Compact grid with AI badges | Industry, layout | AI-powered design suggestions |
| **GoDaddy** | Simplified grid | Industry, business type | Quick-view live site |

### Key Differentiators
- **Webflow** and **Wix** offer the most advanced galleries with component-level interactivity.
- **Carrd** and **Site123** keep galleries intentionally simple to match their positioning.
- **Duda** and **Square Online** emphasize B2B and commerce use cases respectively.

## 3. Pricing Section Layout

### General Trends
- **Comparison table layouts** (per NN/g research) are the dominant pattern.
- **Feature highlighting** via checkmark lists aligned with plan tiers.
- **Visual hierarchy** uses size, color, and spacing to draw attention to the recommended tier.
- **Annual vs monthly toggle** is nearly universal.

### Site-by-Site Observations

| Site | Plans Shown | Starting Price | Annual Discount | Notable Pattern |
|------|------------|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Wix** | Free, Light, Core, Business, Enterprise | Free / $17 mo | ~40% off annual | 5-tier structure; enterprise CTA |
| **Squarespace** | Personal, Business, Basic Comm, Advanced Comm | $16 mo | ~50% off annual | 4 tiers; commerce as separate track |
| **Webflow** | Starter, Basic, Plus, Premium, Enterprise | Free / $14 mo | ~50% off annual | CMS/usage-based pricing tiers |
| **Carrd** | Free, Pro Lite, Pro Standard | $19/yr flat | N/A (annual only) | Simple 3-tier; Pro Standard highlighted |
| **Duda** | Basic, Core, Advanced, Scale | $14 mo | ~40% off annual | Agency-focused team tiers |
| **Square Online** | Connect, Basic, Advanced | Free / $12 mo | ~33% off annual | Commerce transaction fees emphasized |
| **Strikingly** | Free, Super, Pro | Free / $16 mo | ~40% off annual | Feature-gated tiers |
| **Site123** | Free, Premium, Business | Free / $16.80 mo | ~40% off annual | Straightforward 3-tier |
| **Zyro** | Shared, Cloud, Dedicated | Free / ~$3.49 mo | ~50% off annual | Hosting bundle pricing (domain included) |
| **GoDaddy** | Basic, Standard, Advanced | $9.99 mo | ~50% off annual | Integrated hosting/domain upsells |

### Pricing Page Layout Patterns
1. **Tiered card layout** (Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Carrd) -- most common.
2. **Comparison matrix/table** (Duda, GoDaddy) -- better for B2B/agency audiences.
3. **Hosting-bundled pricing** (Zyro) -- domain/hosting included in plan price.
4. **Commerce-separated tracks** (Squarespace, Square Online) -- online store as distinct pricing path.

## 4. Feature Sections

### General Trends
- **Icon + short description** grids (3-4 columns) are the standard pattern.
- **Drag-and-drop builder previews** shown in every site's feature section.
- **AI-powered tools** (copy generation, image generation, design assistance) are now prominently featured across all 10 platforms.
- **Section-based layouts** (hero, features, testimonials, pricing, footer) are consistent.

| **Squarespace** | Visual showcase blocks | Squarespace AI (text, images) | Design-focused; block-based content |
| **Webflow** | Developer/designer dual view | Webflow AI (text, images) | Code export; Figma sync; CMS |
| **Carrd** | Minimal feature list | Limited AI | Simplicity; one-page sites; Pro assets |
| **Duda** | Professional feature grid | Duda AI (responsive design) | Responsive designer; agency tools |

### Feature Section Layout Patterns
- **3-column icon grid** (Wix, Squarespace, Duda, Site123)

## 5. Navigation Structure

### General Trends
- **Sticky top navigation** is universal.
- **Two-tier navigation** (primary + secondary dropdowns) for complex platforms.
- **CTA button in nav** (Sign Up / Get Started) is standard across all 10 sites.
- **Mobile hamburger menus** with simplified navigation.

### Site-by-Site Observations

| Site | Primary Nav Items | Secondary Nav | Sticky | Mobile Pattern |
|------|------------------|---------------|--------|----------------|
| **Wix** | Product, Solutions, Resources, Pricing | Deep dropdowns per item | Yes | Hamburger + full overlay |
| **Squarespace** | Platform, Solutions | Feature sub-pages | Yes | Hamburger + slide panel |
| **Webflow** | Platform, Resources, Pricing | Learning, Community, Showroom | Yes | Hamburger + mega menu |
| **Carrd** | Login, SignUp, Docs, Changelog, Pro, Contact | None | Minimal | Hamburger + compact list |
| **Duda** | Platform, Solutions | Agency, Freelancer, Client | Yes | Hamburger + grouped items |
| **Square Online** | Products, Solutions, Resources | Commerce features | Yes | Hamburger + categories |
| **Strikingly** | Product, Pricing, Blog | Feature pages | Yes | Hamburger + simple list |
| **Site123** | Features, Templates, Pricing | Industry templates | Yes | Hamburger + categories |
| **Zyro** | Hosting, Builder, AI Tools | Plan details | Yes | Hamburger + grouped nav |
| **GoDaddy** | Product, Learn, Resources | Domain, Hosting, Marketing | Yes | Hamburger + mega menu |

### Navigation Patterns
- **Product-focused** (Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Duda) -- emphasizes platform capabilities.
- **Solution-focused** (GoDaddy, Zyro) -- organized by use case (hosting, domain, builder).
- **Minimal** (Carrd) -- 6 items or fewer; docs and changelog for transparency.
- **Commerce-focused** (Square Online) -- product tiers and features lead navigation.

## 6. Footer Design Patterns

### General Trends
- **Multi-column footer designs** are universal across all 10 sites.
- **Extensive link organization** grouped by category (Product, Company, Resources, Legal).
- **Social media links** in a row or column.
- **App download badges** (iOS/Android) increasingly common.
- **Sitemap-friendly** structure with deep linking to help docs and blog posts.

### Site-by-Site Observations

| Site | Columns | Social Links | App Badges | Newsletter | Legal Links |
|------|---------|-------------|------------|------------|-------------|
| **Wix** | 6+ columns | Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn | Yes | Optional | Terms, Privacy, CCPA |
| **Squarespace** | 5 columns | Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube | Yes | Optional | Terms, Privacy, DMCA |
| **Webflow** | 5 columns | Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram | Yes | Optional | Terms, Privacy, Security |
| **Carrd** | 3 compact columns | Twitter, Instagram | No | No | Terms, Privacy |
| **Duda** | 5 columns | LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube | No | No | Terms, Privacy |
| **Square Online** | 5 columns | Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube | Yes | No | Terms, Privacy, Cookie Policy |
| **Strikingly** | 3 columns | Twitter, Facebook, Instagram | No | No | Terms, Privacy |
| **Site123** | 4 columns | Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube | No | Optional | Terms, Privacy |
| **Zyro** | 4 columns | Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube | Yes (Hostinger app) | No | Terms, Privacy |
| **GoDaddy** | 6+ columns | Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn | No | No | Terms, Privacy, Security, Cookies |

### Footer Layout Patterns
1. **Mega-footer** (Wix, GoDaddy, Webflow) -- 5-6 columns with deep link trees. Best for SEO and sitemap coverage.
2. **Standard multi-column** (Squarespace, Duda, Square Online, Site123) -- 4-5 columns. Balanced information density.
3. **Compact footer** (Carrd, Strikingly) -- 2-3 columns. Matches minimalist site positioning.
4. **Brand-integrated footer** (Zyro) -- Hostinger branding visible; cross-product links.

### Common Footer Sections (present on all 10 sites)
- **Product / Platform** -- features, templates, pricing, integrations
- **Company** -- about, careers, press, contact
- **Resources** -- blog, help center, community, docs
- **Legal** -- terms of service, privacy policy, cookie policy, GDPR/CCPA
- **Social** -- 3-5 platform icons
- **App downloads** -- iOS and/or Android badges (6 of 10 sites)

## Summary: Key Takeaways

1. **Split-screen hero sections** dominate high-converting builder sites. Full-screen video is declining; static two-column layouts are preferred for performance.

2. **Template galleries** range from minimalist (Carrd, Site123) to highly interactive (Webflow, Wix). The complexity correlates with the platform's positioning.

3. **Pricing pages** universally use comparison tables or tiered cards. Annual discounts of 40-50% are standard. Commerce platforms separate store pricing from basic site pricing.

4. **AI features** are now a mandatory inclusion in every platform's feature section -- from copy generation to image creation to responsive design assistance.

5. **Navigation** is consistently sticky with a prominent Sign Up / Get Started CTA. Complex platforms use mega-menus; simple platforms keep nav to 6 items or fewer.

6. **Footers** follow a 4-section pattern: Product, Company, Resources, Legal. Social links and app badges are standard additions. Depth of footer links correlates with platform maturity and SEO strategy.
